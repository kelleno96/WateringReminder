❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new plant?

Tap the + button in the top-right corner of the plant list. Enter your plant's name and tap Add.

How do I set up watering reminders?

Tap a plant to open its detail page, then toggle "Remind me to water" on. You can adjust how often you'd like to be reminded using the day stepper.

Can I log a watering I did in the past?

Yes! On the plant detail page, tap "Log a past watering…" and pick the date and time.

What do the colored dots mean?

Green means the plant was recently watered (0–2 days). Orange means it's been a few days (3–6). Red means it's overdue or has never been watered.

Is my data backed up?

Your plant data is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It stays private and is not sent to any server.

The app isn't sending me notifications.

Make sure you've allowed notifications for Watering Reminder in Settings → Notifications → Watering Reminder. Also confirm that reminders are toggled on for the specific plant.
