Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Overview

Watering Reminder ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All plant data — including plant names, watering dates, and reminder settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you when it's time to water your plants. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device. No data is sent to any remote notification service operated by us.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children. Because no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kodevsupport@icloud.com.
